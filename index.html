Did You Know

- 38% Consider the transition to civilian life the most significant challenge for veterans
- 24% Believe finding civilian employment is the greatest challenge
- 40% Of military personnel believe that private companies do not view military experience as a professional asset

Unfortunately

Young veterans regularly observe that the military does an extremely effective job of training them to operate within the military, and an extremely poor job of reversing that training or preparing them before sending them back into civilian life.
